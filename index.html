I am..

This is Yunji, a graduate student at UW-Madison interested in children’s cognition and brain development in the context of education. Especially, I’m interested in the development of nonsymbolic ratio representations and following structural and fuctional brain changes. I do believe different educational experiences shape kid’s brain differently!

I love yoga, netflix, and chocolate.
